FAQs

What programs does Zamzama Government College offer?

Zamzama College offers a variety of programs at the Intermediate, Undergraduate, and Postgraduate levels. These include:

Intermediate (FSc, FA, ICom)

Bachelor's (BA, BSc, BCom)

Master's Programs in various fields (e.g., MA, MSc)

What is the admission process for Zamzama Government College?

The admission process typically includes:

Submission of an online or paper application form.

Submission of required documents (mark sheets, CNIC, photographs, etc.).

An entrance exam or interview (for certain programs).

A merit-based selection process based on previous academic performance.


What is the eligibility criteria for admission?

The eligibility criteria vary based on the program. For example:

Intermediate Programs (FSc, FA, ICom): Generally, students must have completed their Matriculation (10th Grade) or an equivalent exam with a minimum required percentage (varies each year).

Undergraduate Programs (BA, BSc, BCom): Students must have completed their Intermediate education or an equivalent exam.

Postgraduate Programs: Students must have a relevant Bachelor's degree with a minimum percentage as required by the specific department.


Is Zamzama College affiliated with any university?

Yes, Zamzama College is affiliated with the Board of Intermediate Education Karachi (BIEK) for Intermediate programs and with the University of Karachi for undergraduate and postgraduate programs.


What facilities are available at Zamzama College?

Zamzama College provides a range of facilities to ensure a high-quality educational experience. These include:

Modern classrooms with multimedia facilities.

Science laboratories for practical work.

A library with a vast collection of books and digital resources.

Sports facilities for students, including courts and playgrounds.

Computer labs with internet access.

Cafeteria and student lounges for socializing.


What extracurricular activities are offered at Zamzama College?

Zamzama College encourages students to participate in a variety of extracurricular activities to promote leadership and personal growth. These activities include:

Sports competitions (football, cricket, badminton, etc.)

Debates, quizzes, and elocution contests.

Cultural events (theater performances, music, dance, etc.).

Student clubs and societies related to academics, literature, and sports.

Community service and volunteer opportunities.

Does Zamzama College provide career counseling and job placement services?

Yes, Zamzama Government College offers career counseling services to help students make informed career choices. The college also maintains relationships with various organizations for job placement opportunities, internships, and career fairs.


How can I contact the administration or faculty at Zamzama College?

Phone: The contact number for the college office is available on the official website.

College Website: Visit the official website for updates, notices, and online forms.

In-Person: The college's administrative office is open during regular working hours for in-person inquiries.


What are the eligibility criteria for intermediate programs (FSc, FA, ICom)?

FSc (Pre-Medical or Pre-Engineering): Students must have completed their matriculation (SSC) with at least a 60% grade in Science subjects.

FA (General Arts): Students must have completed matriculation with a minimum of 50% marks in Arts or Science subjects.

ICom (Commerce): Students must have completed their matriculation with at least 50% marks in the Commerce or Science stream.
