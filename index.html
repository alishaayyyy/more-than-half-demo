Zamzama College

The college was founded in 1983 to provide quality education to girls in the Karachi area. It was created as part of the governmentâ€™s efforts to promote education for women and girls, especially in urban centers like Karachi, where the demand for such institutions was increasing. Zamzama Government Girls College is affiliated with the Board of Intermediate Education Karachi (BIEK), which means it follows the curriculum set by the board and offers intermediate-level courses. The college also offers undergraduate programs that are recognized by University of Karachi.

Intermediate (Pre-Engineering, Pre-Medical, Commerce, Humanities): The college offers various streams for the intermediate level, including Pre-Engineering, Pre-Medical, Commerce, and Humanities.

Bachelor's Degrees: The college also offers undergraduate programs in Bachelor of Arts (BA) and Bachelor of Science (BSc).

Other Subjects and Vocational Training: The college may also offer short courses or vocational training programs to help students build practical skills.

The mission of Zamzama Government Girls College is to provide affordable, quality education to girls and empower them to achieve success in both academic and professional spheres. The college focuses on nurturing academic excellence, critical thinking, and leadership skills in its students.
